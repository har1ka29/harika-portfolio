01 Business Analytics Project

Objective: To analyse and visualise food wastage patterns within the U.S. retail sector in order to identify key contributors, trends, and areas for potential improvement.

My Contribution: Identified and sourced relevant datasets related to food wastage in the retail sector. Cleaned and prepared the raw data using Tableau Prep Builder, then designed interactive dashboards in Tableau to visualise food wastage across retail sub-sectors, highlight areas with high wastage, and present potential solution-focused insights.

Outcome: The dashboard highlighted major sources of food wastage within the U.S. retail sector, enabling data-driven insights through interactive filters and charts, while also identifying potential solutions for high-wastage sub-sectors.

Tools Used: Tableau Prep Builder, Tableau, Microsoft Excel

03 Financial Management Project

Outcome: The analysis provided data-driven insights into CapitaLand’s financial strength and operational efficiency, highlighting core areas of stability and opportunities for improvement, and supporting informed evaluation of the firm's financial position.
